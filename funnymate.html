FunnyMate

FunnyMate is a comedy duo from Scotland who are known for playing and recording old-school and nostalgic video games such as Lego Harry Potter, LittleBigPlanet, The Simpsons Hit & Run, and more.

Known primarily for their most popular video "We played the LEGO Games In 2022", FunnyMate has embraced some of the current "brainrot" humor and quick editing style, and applied it to their content in order to retain viewer retention.
